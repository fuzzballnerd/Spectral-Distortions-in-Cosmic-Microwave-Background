---

## About

This repository accompanies my ongoing research project on spectral distortions
of the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) at IISc. The project investigates
deviations of the CMB spectrum from a perfect blackbody, covering:

- **μ-type and y-type distortions** and the thermodynamic conditions that produce them
- Energy injection mechanisms — Silk damping of primordial perturbations,
  Bose–Einstein condensation effects, and exotic energy release
- Cosmological implications as probes of the primordial power spectrum,
  the thermal history of the universe, and physics beyond the Standard Model

**Primary tools:** spectroxide, CosmoTherm, Rust, Python (NumPy, SciPy, Pandas), Mathematica
